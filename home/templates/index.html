About

Everyone has felt lonely, at some time or another. Whether its due to a pandemic, or from being surrounded by people that just don't understand you, or because you've just had a baby and you have no one to support you. But don't suffer, you are not alone!

Everyday people are struggling, whether it be loneliness, depression or anxiety there are so many people feeling the same!
We decided to bring you a space for us all to get the support, understanding and connections we're lacking and to ultimately be heard.
Connectify is here to help you build meaningful connections in our community.
Register to take our quiz and to connect with and support others in our community blog.
